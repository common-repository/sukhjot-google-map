Auto Poster

Installation instructions

  • 1.Upload the auto_poster plugin to your wordpress plugin directory.
  • 2.Then go to your wp-admin panel and under the plugins list activate the Auto Poster plugin.
  • 3.After activation under the Settings menu you will see a new menu called "Auto Poster".
  • 4.Make the .zip file of your files please don't add any folder in it. and follow the steps and your files will be upload on the site as a post.
  • 5.Done
